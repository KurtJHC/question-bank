Writing Task
 

 

Use the sentence below to begin a narrative. Include an orientation, a complication and resolution.

It was as if I had nothing better to do. I was stressed as it was, about Dad seeing the parcel we were due to receive today. On top of that, my teacher kept asking me to run errands for her. I knew I had to trick her and escape class. If I didn’t hide that parcel, I’d be in big trouble.


You will be judged on:
   • What you have to say,
   • How well you organise the way you say it, and
   • How clearly and effectively you express yourself.
