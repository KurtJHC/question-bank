For some people, it is seen as “wrecking kids” or even “killing parents.” These people are right. Without homework, the life of both students, and even parents, would be way better. 

Here’s why: students are given way too much homework. Students who spend too much time on homework are not always able to meet other needs, like being physically and socially active. 

Which of the following best expresses the flaw in the above argument? 
 
  1.  The author has not proved that people still argue over the necessity of homework.
  2.  The author has not proved that without homework, the life of both students and even parents would be way better. 
  3.  If students are given too much homework, you could give them less homework without banning homework.
  4.  We are not told just how much homework time would interfere with a student’s physical and social development.
