The only real limitation of power is the natural consequences of the principles themselves. {^ Extract B ^}
Habits are powerful factors in our lives. Because they are consistent, often unconscious patterns, they constantly, daily, express our character and produce our effectiveness... or ineffectiveness. As Horace Mann, the great educator, once said, “Habits are like a cable. But I also know it isn’t a quick fix. It involves a process and tremendous commitment.

Dependent people need others to get what they want. Independent people can get what they want through their own effort. Interdependent people combine their own efforts with the efforts of others to achieve their greatest success.
