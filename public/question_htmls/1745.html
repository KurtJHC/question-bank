Out of all the things you’re actively doing on stage, that only controls about 50% of what other people think of you. The other 50% is unspoken and comes from your body language. That means you can’t stand there like a statue behind your instrument or the mic stand. You need to be moving to the music. Don’t fidget about. Don’t cross your arms. Don’t sit down.
