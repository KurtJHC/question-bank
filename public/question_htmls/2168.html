The Southern Residents were chosen to be part of the Species in the Spotlight program to rescue the population from near "certain" extinction. NOAA started the Species in the Spotlight program to provide targeted, immediate efforts to halt the decline and stabilise populations of the Southern Resident orcas and several other species thought to be headed for extinction in the United States.

Writing task

Imagine that you are at a five-day school camp for your year group. Each student has to perform in one of the entertainment variety concerts in the evening. For your role, you have been asked to tell your year group an interesting story that may be happy or sad or in-between.

Write down the informal speech that you plan to tell the group, when it is your turn.

In your written speech you may

  • use an event that happened to you as the basis of your story.
  • change or find new details to make your story more interesting.
  • make comments to keep your audience involved.
  • have other characters (but not too many).

Your writing will be assessed on

  • what you have to say.
  • how well you organise and structure your ideas.
  • how effectively and clearly you express yourself.

Writing task
