Stressed Turkey Colours

 

A turkey has several flaps of loose skin around the head and neck that change colour depending on its mood. The flaps are called the "wattle" (on the chin), the "snood" (on the head and over the beak) and the "caruncle" (on the throat). Under calm conditions, these areas are pinkish-grey or blue; if the turkey is annoyed or mating, all these areas turn bright red.

If a turkey is being held down, about to be slaughtered, predict what colour a turkey’s wattle would be.

  1. Pinkish grey.

  2. Red and black.

  3. Bright red.

  4. Dark red.
