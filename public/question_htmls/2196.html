HOMEWORK

Below is a list of the advantages of extracurricular activities. Read these advantages and use them to write an exposition essay arguing that - Extracurricular Activities Should Be Compulsory at Schools.

NOTE: You do not have to use all of these arguments. This is like a brain storm list. Link similar ideas together and choose the three strongest arguments to write about.

  Academic Performance ~
Studies show that students involved in extracurricular activities perform better academically due to enhanced time management and discipline

  Holistic Development ~
Develops social, emotional and leadership skills outside academics. 

  Physical and Mental Wellbeing ~
Many activities can help reduce stress through the arts and promote physical fitness through sports.

  Teamwork and Collaboration ~
Provides opportunities for students to work in teams, fostering communication, cooperation and conflict resolution skills.

  Exploring Interests ~
Helps students discover and develop talents or passions outside of curriculum. 

  College and Career Prospects ~
Extracurricular activities are valued by colleges and employers as it shows a well-rounded personality. 

  Social Inclusion and Belonging ~
Students build friendships and a sense of belonging, promoting inclusivity.

  Reduce Risky Behaviours ~
Keeps students engaged in constructive activities and reduces likelihood of engaging in harmful or risky behaviour.

  Cultural Appreciation ~
Creative and Cultural clubs encourage appreciation of arts and diversity, fostering cultural awareness.

  Community Engagement ~
Volunteering and Environmental clubs connect students to community and promote civic responsibility. 

  Lifelong Skills ~
Provides practical skills such as public speaking, time management and leadership.
