Learn the following words and their meanings.

 

1. frontier - the part of a country which borders another country

2. opaque - not clear; hard to understand

3. diffuse - to spread or scatter widely or thinly

4. deviate - to turn aside

5. parachute - an apparatus used in descending safely through the air esp. from an aircraft

6. encroach - to trespass upon the property or rights of another

7. aberrant - straying from the right or usual course

8. barnacle - small shellfish that fix themselves tightly to rocks and bottoms of boats

9. defensive - serving to protect and defend

10. festive - joyful, merry

11. emporium - a large store selling a great number of items

12. insinuate - to suggest or hint slyly

13. intense - of an extreme kind

14. abeyance - temporary inactivity or suspension

15. mattock - an instrument for loosening soil

16. deflate - to release the gas or air from something

17. heckle - to badger or torment; to harass

18. elapse - to slip or pass away esp. referring to time

19. paraphernalia - miscellaneous equipment needed for a particular activity.

20. artillery - mounted guns that are moveable or stationary

21. mechanic - a skilled worker with tools or machines

22. bazaar - a marketplace

23. capacious - capable of holding a lot

24. fatigue - weariness from bodily or mental exercise

25. retrieve - to recover or regain

26. stupendous - such as to cause amazement

27. ascertain - to find out by trial, examination or experiment, so as to determine

28. pittance - a small allowance or sum for living expenses

29. terminus - the station or town at the end of a railway line, bus route etc.

30. testify - to bear witness; to give evidence esp. in a courtroom
