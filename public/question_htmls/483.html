Writing Task

 

Kim and William have been asked by their parents to choose where they would like to go for their next holiday. Kim would like to visit the Great Wall of China. However William wants to go on a safari in Africa.


Imagine that you are either Kim or William.
Write an exposition supporting your choice of a holiday destination.

 

You will be judged on how well you structure and write your argument, and how well you express your opinion.
